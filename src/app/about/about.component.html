About Us

Nodebucket is here to help you. With many projects created together with many happy users so far, we would like nothing more than to help you along your journey. Whether you be from house Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, or anywhere else between, we would be our pleasure to assist you.

Driven through passion

Founded in 2024 by students from Bellevue University, Nebraska, Nodebucket was created through a concerted effort and goal of helping others achieve their dreams and passions. We thought to ourselves. "How can we help passionate people chase their dreams". That along with many discussions among individuals who dared to dream big led us all to one conclusion. Why not use what we know best? Of course being developers, that meant applications and development. If anybody needed a hand gently pushing them along, then we wanted that hand to be us. With our task management system, we aimed to create a system that would aid in time management while also providing consulting work for those who needed it, and while our journey has led us into helping so many so far we know that this is only the beginning, and we can't wait to go further and farther beyond.
